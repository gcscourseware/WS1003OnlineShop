1. Platform-Based vs. Independent E-commerce

Choosing your e-commerce model is the first critical step. Use the tabs below to explore the differences.

Platform-Based E-commerce

  • What it is: Selling within a larger, established marketplace with pre-built traffic and infrastructure.
  • Key Pro: Instant access to millions of potential customers.
  • Key Con:Less control over branding and customer data;high competition.

Independent E-commerce (Self-Hosted)

  • What it is:Building your own dedicated online store with a custom domain.
  • Key Pro:Full brand control, ownership of customer data, and high customization.
  • Key Con:Requires driving your own traffic; higher initial setup and marketing effort.

2. Popular E-commerce Platforms Overview

Explore some of the world's most popular platforms and their primary focus.

Shopify

Primary Focus: Independent Store Building.Provides tools for merchants to create their own customizable online stores.

Amazon

Primary Focus: Massive Global Marketplace.Unmatched reach and logistics for a massive range of products.

Shopee & Lazada

Primary Focus: Southeast Asian Marketplace.Dominant in the SEA region, known for mobile-first shopping and promotions.

Etsy

Primary Focus: Unique/Handmade Goods.A niche marketplace for vintage, handmade, and craft supplies.

TikTok Shop

Primary Focus: Social Commerce/Live Selling.Integrates e-commerce directly into the social media experience.
