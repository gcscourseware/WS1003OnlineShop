Mastering the fundamentals of launching an online business.

üéØ 1. Key Planning Factors

Definition: The specific group of consumers most likely to buy your product. Failing to define this leads to wasted marketing and poor product fit.

  • Demographics: Age, income, location, education.
  • Psychographics: Hobbies, values, lifestyle, purchasing behavior.

Key Models: Defines how you create, deliver, and capture value.

  • B2C (Business-to-Consumer): Selling directly to the end-user (e.g., selling a t-shirt).
  • D2C (Direct-to-Consumer): Manufacturer/Brand sells directly to consumers, bypassing retailers (e.g., Warby Parker).
  • B2B (Business-to-Business):Selling to other companies (e.g., selling industrial equipment).
  • Dropshipping:Selling products without holding inventory.

Impact: Determines logistics, payment requirements, and platform features needed.

  • Physical Goods: Requires inventory management, shipping, and fulfillment tools.
  • Digital Products: No shipping, requires secure downloads/streaming tools (e.g., e-books, software).
  • Services: Requires booking/scheduling tools and potentially recurring billing.

2. Cost Structures

The fixed monthly cost for using a hosted platform (like Shopify or BigCommerce). These costs are predictable and often scale up for features like advanced reporting or lower transaction fees.

Choosing between hosted (e.g., Shopify) and self-hosted (e.g., WooCommerce) options involves balancing these three factors:

Feature Hosted Platform (e.g., Shopify) Self-Hosted (e.g., WooCommerce)
Ease of Use High:Quick setup, drag-and-drop, low technical skill required. Low-Moderate: Requires managing hosting, updates, and more technical setup.
Scalability High: Excellent for fast growth; platform handles all server resources. Unlimited: Depends entirely on your chosen web host and server resources.
Customization Moderate:Limited by platform's core code and API access. Good for standard stores. Near-Infinite: Full control over the underlying code. Best for unique or complex features.
