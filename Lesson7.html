Google favors fast, mobile-friendly sites. Ensure your store:

  1. Loads quickly (Optimize image file sizes).
  2. Is fully responsive on mobile devices.
  3. Has a clear site structure (easy for bots to crawl).

External links pointing to your store's product pages signal authority to search engines. Strategies include:

  • **Guest Posting:** Writing articles for relevant industry blogs.
  • **Press Coverage:** Getting featured on news sites.
  • **Affiliate Marketing:** Partnering with others who link to your products.

Promotions create excitement and urgency, driving immediate sales, but must be managed carefully to protect profit margins.

  • **Percentage Discount (e.g., 20% off):** Simple and widely used. Best for driving volume.
  • **Dollar Amount Discount (e.g., $10 off):** Effective for lower-priced items, as the perceived value is higher.
  • **Free Gift/Buy X Get Y:** Great for moving inventory or increasing the Average Order Value (AOV).
  • **Conditional Sales:** Only applies to specific customer segments (e.g., "First-time buyers only").

Use time-bound sales to prevent procrastination:

Flash Sale: A steep discount that lasts for a very short period (e.g., 4 hours). Requires high-volume traffic to be effective. Always use timers on the product page to enforce the deadline.

Also, use "limited stock" notifications to trigger scarcity buying behavior.

Before running a sale, verify your break-even point. Ensure the promotion still covers your Cost of Goods Sold (COGS) and operational overhead.

üì± 3. Social Media and Influencer Partnerships

Social channels are crucial for building brand identity and fostering direct customer relationships.

Your content should follow the 80/20 rule:

  • **80% Value:** Entertaining, educating, or inspiring content (e.g., tutorials, behind-the-scenes, tips).
  • **20% Promotion:** Direct sales posts, link drops, or explicit ads.
[Image of a typical social media marketing funnel]

Focus on video (Reels, TikTok) as it generates the highest engagement rates.

Partnering with relevant content creators allows you to access a trusted, niche audience.

Micro-Influencers: Those with 1,000 to 10,000 highly engaged followers often provide a better Return on Investment (ROI) and more authentic engagement than large celebrities.
